Our Street Nanaimo

A vision for a more vibrant and pedestrian friendly Commercial Street in Downtown Nanaimo

A pedestrianized street in the heart of Nanaimo!

Open Streets

Open Streets programs, also known as Ciclovias, open streets to people by closing them to automobiles, so people may enjoy them more fully. Our Street Nanaimo is a tactical urbanism intiative to close Commercial Street from Bastion Street to Terminal Avenue.

New Oppurtunities

Open Streets programs are a stimulus to local businesses and they provide new opportunities for civic participation. Our Street Nanaimo is an advocacy pilot project with the goal of creating a more vibrant and lively downtown Nanaimo.
