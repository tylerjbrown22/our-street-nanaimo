An Open Street

Open Streets programs open streets to people by closing them to automobiles, so people may enjoy them more fully. Open Streets are a stimulus to local businesses and provide new opportunities for civic participation.

About Our Street Nanaimo

Our Street Nanaimo is an advocacy intiative to improve pedestrian and cycling infrastructure in Nanaimo. We believe large meaningful change can start from small tactical urbanism projects.

Improving the sustainability, vitality and liveability of Nanaimo happens one street, one block and one neighbourhood at a time. We are interested in the problems you identify and the solutions you dream of, so please get in touch..
