Our Street Nanaimo

Our Street Nanaimo is an Open Streets advocacy initiative to improve the downtown core of Nanaimo. We believe closing Commercial Street to automobile traffic will enliven the downtown, support local business and create a memorable experience for visiting tourists. Our goal is to instigate meaningful and positive change from small tactical urbanism projects.

Improving the sustainability, vitality and livability of Nanaimo happens one street, one block and one neighbourhood at a time. Commercial Street is an important centre of public life in Nanaimo. Our Street Nanaimo proposes to close Commercial Street from Bastion Street to Terminal Avenue (Island Highway) for the summer months in 2018.

Open Streets

Open Streets programs close streets to automobiles so people may enjoy them more fully. Open Streets are a stimulus to local businesses and provide new opportunities for civic participation.

Tactical Urbanism

“Tactical urbanism” is a deliberate approach to city-making that uses short-term action to instigate long-term change. Approaches include pop-up parks, pavement to plazas and better block intiatives.
